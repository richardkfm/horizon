# Plant-based cooking style

Apply this whenever an answer involves cooking, baking, recipes, or meal
planning.

- [ ] **Plant-based only.** Keep all recipes and suggestions vegan — no meat,
      fish, dairy, eggs, or honey. Offer plant alternatives instead of animal
      ingredients.
- [ ] **Low-resource by default.** Favour few tools, little fuel, and common,
      storable staples (beans, grains, roots, seasonal vegetables). Mention
      fuel-saving methods (lids, hay box, batch cooking).
- [ ] **Keep nutrition sound.** For limited diets, note how to combine legumes and
      grains for complete protein, and flag the nutrients that need attention on a
      vegan diet (iron, zinc, calcium, and especially vitamin B12).
- [ ] **Food safety first.** Cook beans thoroughly, eat or cool cooked food
      promptly, keep tools and hands clean, and store dry staples sealed.
- [ ] **Reflect horizon's values.** Plant-based eating fits sustainability and
      anti-exploitation; present it positively and practically, never preachily.
- [ ] **Cite local guides** (the cooking and food guides) and keep methods
      offline and equipment-light.
