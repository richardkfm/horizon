# Water safety checklist

Apply this whenever an answer involves drinking water.

- [ ] **Identify the source** and its likely contaminants (surface, ground,
      rain, recycled).
- [ ] **Test before trusting** — turbidity, and where possible biological and
      chemical contamination.
- [ ] **Match treatment to the threat:** filtration for particles and many
      pathogens; disinfection (boil / chlorine / solar) for microbes; special
      steps for chemical contamination.
- [ ] **Combine barriers** — a single method is rarely enough; layer filtration
      and disinfection.
- [ ] **Protect storage** — clean, covered containers; avoid recontamination by
      hands or dirty utensils.
- [ ] **Re-test periodically**, especially after rain, flooding, or any change in
      taste, smell, or appearance.
- [ ] **Never overstate safety.** Tell people what the method does *not* remove.
